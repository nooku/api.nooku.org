# _createNonce( string $secret, integer $timeout = 180 )

Generate a Nonce.

Generate a Nonce.

The generated nonce will contains three parts, separated by a colon. The first part is the individual salt. The second part is the time until the nonce is valid. The third part is a HMAC hash of the salt, the time, and a secret value.
