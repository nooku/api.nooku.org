Class KObjectStack

Object Stack

A stack is a data type or collection in which the principal (or only) operations on the collection are the addition of an object to the collection, known as push and removal of an entity, known as pop. The relation between the push and pop operations is such that the stack is a Last-In-First-Out (LIFO) data structure.

KObject implements KObjectInterface, KObjectMixable, KObjectHandlable, KObjectDecoratable
KObjectStack implements Iterator, Countable, Serializable
